### Step 1: Open Settings
1. Click your profile picture in the top-right corner

3. Scroll down to find "Notifications"

1. Click the "Enable Notifications" button

1. Open another window/tab with another account
2. Send a message to yourself

## ‚ùì Common Questions

### Why isn't it showing notifications?

**Check these things:**

1. **Settings - Is it enabled?**
   - Go to Settings ‚Üí Notifications
   - Button should show "Disable Notifications"

2. **Browser permissions - Did you click Allow?**
   - Click the lock icon next to the URL
   - Look for "Notifications"
   - Should show "Allow"

3. **Focus - Is the app in focus?**
   - Minimize or close the Chatter window
   - Then send a test message
   - The notification should appear

4. **Browser support - Are you using a modern browser?**
   - Chrome ‚úÖ Works great
   - Firefox ‚úÖ Works great
   - Edge ‚úÖ Works great
   - Safari ‚ö†Ô∏è Limited support

### Can I turn off notifications later?

**Yes!** Go to Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Click "Disable Notifications"

### Will notifications drain my battery?

**No**, notifications are very efficient. They're processed by your browser/OS, not the app.

### Are my messages being stored or shared?

**No**, only subscription details are stored (encrypted). Messages are not stored.

### Can I get notifications for specific people?

Not yet, but we're working on it! Currently you get notifications for all conversations.

## üõ†Ô∏è Troubleshooting

### "Permission Denied" Message

1. Go to Settings ‚Üí Browser Settings
2. Look for Chatter or the website
3. Change Notifications permission to "Always Allow"
4. Try again

### "Notifications not supported"

This means your browser doesn't support notifications. Try:
- Google Chrome (best support)
- Mozilla Firefox
- Microsoft Edge
- Opera

### Notifications stopped working

Try these steps in order:

1. **Clear browser cache**
   - Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Clear browsing data
   - Check "Cookies and other site data"
   - Click "Clear data"

2. **Disable and re-enable**
   - Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Disable
   - Wait 5 seconds
   - Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí Enable
   - Grant permission again

3. **Check browser permissions**
   - Lock icon in address bar
   - Verify Notifications is "Allow"

4. **Restart browser**
   - Close all Chatter windows
   - Reopen the app
   - Try enabling again
